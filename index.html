About Me

My story

I was born on the Tanzanian slopes of Mount Kilimanjaro. From an early age, I was fascinated by technology. I became somewhat addicted to disassembling and reassembling gadgets in order to better understand their working mechanisms.

In the first months of my junior year of secondary school, I discovered that I had a passion for Information and communication technologies. While some were turned away by its complexity, I was drawn in and motivated to learn ways of communicating with computers, as a result, I taught myself C/C++, python, and am now beginning to explore Java and Javascript

Outside the realm of technology, I love spending my free time on Reddit, it's so fun reading what people have in their minds! My favourite subreddits are:
 r/ Showerthoughts, r/Abstractart, r/technicallythetruth and r/Imaginarysliceoflife.
I also love gaming, specifically Minecraft. My favourite Music genres are: LoFi hip-hop and Amapiano instrumentals , and my favourite colors are any shades of blues, dark shades of yellows and greens.
